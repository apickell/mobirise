What am I all about?

Education

Undergraduate & Graduate Certificate

My business and technology degree allows me to understand multiple aspects of the industry such as finance, marketing, management, etc. With my two concentrations of computer science and marketing, I take additional classes to gain a deeper comprehension of the subject matter. I picked up a graduate certificate of project management my sophomore year to ensure that I am prepared for handling people and projects in the future.

Experience

2 Years in Leadership Roles

The Delta Phi Epsilon sorority has enhanced my college experience in many ways, including boosting my leadership capabilities. As a sophomore, my first position was on the leadership team as Vice President of House Management. In this role, I was involved in housing selection, resident safety and satisfaction, administrative duties, and making executive decisions. More recently, I was elected New Member Educator to coordinate the involvment of 100 sisters and new members, including a process committee, to assure the adequate education of the new members. 

Extras

Involved On & Off Campus

Besides Delta Phi Epsilon, I am involved throughout campus and off-campus. As a business school student ambassador, I assist in the recruitment of new students to Stevens and the business school specifically. In addition to showing my pride, I am a member of StevensThon- a 12-hour dance marathon to benefit Children's Specialized Hospital. Finaly, off-campus I have been travelling to the Dominican Republic and Peru over the last 4 summers with Groups Of Extraordinary Students (G.O.E.S.). As a group, we build houses for poverty-stricken families.
