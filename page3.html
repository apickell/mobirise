College and Beyond 

Here is a timeline from the beginning of my college career to where I hope to be in the future. 

August 2015

Freshman Computer Scientist

As a first-year college student, I completed core classes and got a taste of computer science. During second semester, was enrolled in a project management class. I felt more cognitively stimulated and freely creative. Because of this course, I changed my major to business and technology to begin the upcoming August. 

June 2016

Dominican Republic Mission Trip

For the third summer in a row, I was returning to the Dominican Republic to help the poverty-stricken village in any way I could. The group I travelled with, G.O.E.S., consisted of 30 students in addition to our three organization leaders. Together, we worked to build four houses for the chosen families. 

August 2016

Sophomore Business & Technology Major

When it came time to declare a business and a technology concentration, computer science was a no-brainer, but it was difficult to decide between marketing and management. After a bit of research, I discovered that it was possible to take some graduate courses over my college career in order to obtain a graduate certificate. I declared my business concentration as marketing, and began taking courses for a project management graduate certificate. 

May 2017

Research Intern at P.G. Chambers School

A team of four students, including myself, took on a project for P.G. Chambers School- a school for children with special needs from kindergarten to 8th grade. We re-wired the foot pedals of children's ride-along cars and created a push button instead. This is a cheaper and more enjoyable alternative to a wheelchair for children with little-to-no leg mobility. In order to provide the materials, we gathered donations for the research. Our advisor recently received a grant for the project which we used to purchase more cars for the school to use in their physical therapy program. 

July 2017

Peru Mission Trip

To begin a community in a new place, G.O.E.S. travelled to La Florida, Peru to build three houses over the summer. We worked alongside the locals and grew new relationships with the children and adults throughout the town. 

July 2017

Teaching Assistant at Stevens

Stevens Institute of Technology offers a week-long program for high school students to experience a specific subject matter on campus. These subjects ranged from engineering to business to the arts. I assisted the entrepreneurship teacher throughout the week. I coordinated site visits to emerging entrepreneurial companies and established relations with each student. I also provided my own personal experiences to ensure their success and to enrich the studentsâ€™ learning experience of potential career opportunities. 

June 2018

RBC Capital Markets Operations Analyst Intern

As an intern at Royal Bank of Canada Capital Markets (RBCCM), I was given various responsibilities relating to the many areas of support for the capital markets division, as well as exposure to the products and services offered by the investment bank. I was able to obtain hands-on experience working alongside experienced business professionals and gain exposure to the different areas of capital markets and an understanding of RBC Capital Markets capabilities.

May 2019

Stevens Graduate

As a graduate of Stevens, I hope to solitify a full-time job in New Jersey that will utilize the skills obtained throughout the past four years. 

May 2024

Established Career

Five years after graduation, I hope to have found the job I enjoy most and turned it into a career. Moving up within the company can help me begin my adult life and acquire useful knowledge and experiences.
